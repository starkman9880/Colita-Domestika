Colita

The pioneer who captured 20th century Spain in her photographs
Learn from Colita

Patience and perseverance. According to Colita, these are the secrets to becoming a good photographer. Isabel Steva Hernández, also known as Colita, is a photographer from Barcelona who has dedicated her life to photographing the faces, stories, and prominent figures of the 20th century.

In this episode of Domestika Maestros, she shares the secrets behind her career, which she began by photographing prominent artists in the world of flamenco and then developed by capturing day-to-day life in her beloved city of Barcelona. Take a tour of her studio as she discusses her career and the secrets to achieving the best photographs.
